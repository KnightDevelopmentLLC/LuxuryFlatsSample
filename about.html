Who We Are

Redefining
Luxury Living

Elysia Residences was born from a single, unwavering conviction — that a home is not merely a structure, but a lived experience. Founded in 2009 by Arvind Mehta, a visionary architect turned developer, we have spent 15 years transforming Hyderabad's skyline one extraordinary residence at a time.

"We build for people who understand that true luxury lies not in excess, but in the perfect detail."

From our very first project in Jubilee Hills to our current portfolio spanning Hyderabad's most sought-after addresses, every Elysia home carries our signature promise: uncompromising quality, thoughtful design, and a lifestyle that rewards the senses.

Arvind Mehta Founder & Chairman, Elysia Residences
